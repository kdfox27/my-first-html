How to Make French Pressed Coffee

The French press is my prefered brew method, because its an excellent balance between the cost of the equipment and
taste of the coffee. Here's how you create the perfect cup:

  1. Grind 2 tbs of coffee per 6oz of water
  2. Pour required amount of hot water (just under boiling temperature) over grounds
  3. Let grounds steep for 4 - 5 minutes
  4. Press with strainer, pour, and enjoy
